# Task 3: Monthly Charges Distribution

## Objective

The objective of this task is to analyze the distribution of customer monthly charges using a histogram and identify pricing patterns across the customer base.

---

## Activities Performed

### 1. Monthly Charges Analysis

* Examined the MonthlyCharges column.
* Created a histogram using intervals of 10.
* Analyzed the distribution of customer monthly charges.

### 2. Chart Formatting

* Added chart title.
* Added axis labels.
* Added data labels for better readability.

### 3. Business Interpretation

* Identified the most common pricing ranges.
* Investigated charge distribution patterns.

---

## Key Findings

* Most customers fall within the $18.25–$28.25 monthly charge range.
* A total of 1,606 customers belong to this range.
* A second concentration of customers is observed between $78.25 and $98.25.
* Very few customers have monthly charges above $118.
* Monthly charges vary significantly across customers.

---

## Business Question

Does a higher monthly charge increase the likelihood of customer churn?

### Current Status

Further analysis is required to determine the relationship between Monthly Charges and customer churn.
