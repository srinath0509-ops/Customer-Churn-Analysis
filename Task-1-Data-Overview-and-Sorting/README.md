# Task 1: Data Overview and Simple Sorting

## Objective

The objective of this task is to understand the structure of the Telecom Customer Churn dataset and prepare it for further visualization and analysis.

---

## Dataset Information

Metric           Values     
-------------- - ---------
Total Records  - 7044      
Total Features - 21        
Minimum Tenure - 0 Months  
Maximum Tenure - 72 Months 
Missing Values - 0         

---

## Activities Performed

### 1. Dataset Exploration

* Explored the dataset structure.
* Identified customer demographics, service details, billing information, and churn status.
* Examined the available Internet Service and Contract categories.

### 2. Tenure Analysis

* Sorted customer records in ascending order of tenure.
* Sorted customer records in descending order of tenure.
* Identified newly joined customers and long-term customers.

### 3. Churn Filtering

* Applied filters to display only churned customers.
* Analyzed churn-specific records for further investigation.

---

## Key Findings

* The dataset contains 7044 customer records and 21 attributes.
* Customer tenure ranges from 0 to 72 months.
* The dataset contains no missing values.
* Internet Service categories include DSL, Fiber Optic, and No Internet Service.
* Contract categories include Month-to-Month, One Year, and Two Year plans.
* A total of 1869 customers have churned.
* A total of 5174 customers remain active.
* Customer churn rate is 26.53%.
* Customer retention rate is 73.47%.

---

## Business Questions for Further Analysis

* Do customers with lower tenure churn more frequently?
* Does MonthlyCharges influence customer churn?
* Which contract type experiences the highest churn rate?
* Does Internet Service type affect customer retention?
